Who is this actually for?
Men roughly 35 to 55 who've noticed something shifting — sleep, energy, drive, body composition, mood — and don't want to wait until it becomes a doctor's problem. You don't need to be starting from zero. You don't need to be an elite athlete. You need to be willing to run a real 30-day experiment on yourself.
Do I need gym equipment?
No. Protocols are built to scale from bodyweight-at-home to a fully kitted gym. Strength work is central, but "strength work" means progressive load on compound movements — whatever tools you have will work.
Is this a strength program or an endurance program?
Neither, and both. Movement is one of six pillars — and the movement pillar combines strength, zone 2 cardio, and mobility because that combination is what the midlife body actually needs. If you want a pure hypertrophy program, this isn't it.

Two things. First, it's built around the actual physiology of men in midlife — not a generic template. Second, every protocol ships with the reasoning behind it, because we don't follow inputs we don't understand, and we shouldn't. No guru energy. No mystique. Just the work.
